\begin{command}[@FWR/transform]{transform}	\desc{Purpose}Perform a UYW-transformation (similarity on Z)	\desc{Syntax}\matlab{R = transform(R, U, Y, W)}	\desc{Parameters}		\begin{tabular}{l@{\ :\ }p{9cm}}\matlab{R} &  FWR object                  \\\matlab{U,Y,W } &  transformation matrices\\		\end{tabular}	\desc{Description}The $\mt{U}\mt{Y}\mt{W}$-transformation is defined as a particular similarity on $Z$:\begin{equation}\tilde{Z} =\begin{pmatrix}\mt{Y}\\&\mt{U}^{-1}\\&&I_{p}\end{pmatrix}Z\begin{pmatrix}\mt{W}\\&\mt{U}\\&&I_{m}\end{pmatrix}\end{equation}where $\mt{U}$, $\mt{W}$, $\mt{Y}$ are non-singular matrices, areequivalent to $\mathcal{R}$.\end{command}
