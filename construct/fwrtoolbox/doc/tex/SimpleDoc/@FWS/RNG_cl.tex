\begin{command}[@FWS/RNGcl]{RNG\_cl}	\desc{Purpose}Compute the closed-loop Roundoff Noise Gain for a FWS.The computation is based on the UYW-transform	\desc{Syntax}\matlab{G = RNG\_cl(S,U,Y,W, Plant, tol)}	\desc{Parameters}		\begin{tabular}{l@{\ :\ }p{9cm}}\matlab{G} &  roundoff noise gain                             \\\matlab{S} &  FWS object                                      \\\matlab{U,Y,W } &  transformation matrices                    \\\matlab{Plant} &  ss of the plant                             \\\matlab{tol} &  tolerance on trivial parameters (default=1e-8)\\		\end{tabular}	\desc{Description}This function computes the closed-loop Roundoff Noise Gain for a FWS. The computation is based on the UYW-transform.\\It is based on the \funcName[@FWR/RNGcl]{RNG\_cl} function.\\The matrix $d_Z$ is computed once and stored in the\matlab{dataMeasure} field.\desc{See also}\funcName[@FWS/RNG]{RNG}, \funcName[@FWR/RNGcl]{RNG\_cl}	\desc{References}\cite{Hila08b} T. Hilaire, P. Chevrel, and J. Whidborne. Finite wordlength controller realizations using the specialized implicit form. Technical Report RR-6759, INRIA, 2008.\end{command}
